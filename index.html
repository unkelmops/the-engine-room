How does MOPSENTRY EA work?

When you attach MOPSENTRY EA to a currency pair or index, it first checks the overall trend direction on the 4-hour timeframe to determine whether the market is bullish or bearish.

After confirming the bias, it does not place trades immediately. It waits for a specific market movement or volatility condition before executing any orders. This is the behavior in Version 2.

Version 1 (build 351) places orders right away when attached to the chart. Version 2 is coming soon — it will wait for the right setup instead of trading instantly.
